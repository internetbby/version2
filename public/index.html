Solar Age

Explore the Cosmos with your crew

Developed by Original Mirror Arts, Solar Age is a groundbreaking first person space exploration pirate simulator. Whether a far off mountain or a planet hanging moo on the skyline, you'll be able go there. Where you’ll go and how quick you’ll make your way through this universe is up to you. It’s yours for the taking. Investigate a endless open Cosmos of untainted planets, lost ships and puzzling antiques. Look for misplaced treasure, lost alien species. Go up against reviled space Captains and secure valuable space minerals.
